About the Book

Stand at the edge of everything you thought you knew.

When the comfort of routine crumbles and the familiar landscape dissolves, you find yourself standing at The Edge. In this new reality, the horizon beckons with a wild promise — one must learn to see with unclouded eyes.

Prepare to confront the monolith of perception, to dismantle the internal filters that have long defined your existence. This is not a journey for the faint of heart, but for the sovereign mind — one who dares to step into direct, factual reality and find their unshakeable centre.

A new art of living begins on this precipice. Step into the direct, factual reality and find your unshakeable center.

— The Edge, back cover
